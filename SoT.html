Sea Of Thieves

2018 - 2026

Working with Rare on all of their cinematic trailers(it must be over 20 by now!) from previs to final frames. The process would require our team to extract game assets from Rares perforce, modify props and re-rig characters for our pipeline & animators then intergrate back into their version of unreal.

We'd take temporary assets and block previs scenes in maya, moving over to unreal to produce the next stage of previs where final cameras and cuts would be locked in sequencer. We'd then continue to scene & look dev custom environments, modify props and make bespoke VFX, these would then all be lit in the in unreal where we would pull back the optimisation limitations and tweak post prcoessing to produce striking visuals.

Tools used: Unreal Engine 4 / Maya / Photoshop / houdini / Premier / Nuke

My role: Layout / Setup / Previs / Asset work / Lighting /Scene & look dev / Final frame rendering / Edit, Output + Comp where required
