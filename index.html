about

I’m a 3D Generalist specialising in Unreal Engine - creating cinematic experiences for both real-time and pre-rendered pipelines.

I’m a multidisciplinary artist with experience working across game and television productions, cinematics, asset creation and on-set support in both pre/post-vis. While my core focus is 3D and Unreal Engine, my broader creative background includes UI design, 2D artwork, illustration, and creative design.

My background as a generalist allows me to move comfortably between environment creation, cinematic layout, lighting, and technical setup, while keeping a strong focus on storytelling and visual clarity.

cinematic work

My 3D work focuses on cinematics, pre/post-vis, and virtual production using Unreal Engine. I’ve worked across game and television projects creating cinematic sequences, shot layouts, environments, and on-set support to help teams explore and refine visual storytelling and vfx before production.
